Introduction
  • Transparency: Kari emphasizes her focus on transparency, noting the council's efforts to enhance communication through social media, public GIS maps, and updates after every council meeting. She supports annual State of the City updates and infographics to ensure residents know city accomplishments and priorities.
  • Community Engagement and Equity: Kari highlights her support for New Brighton's Equity Commission, aiming to create an inclusive community. She emphasizes the importance of active listening and meaningful engagement, describing the city's work to reflect the community's diverse values.
  • Housing: She advocates for a variety of housing options, informed by the city's first housing study. She supports policies like short-term rental ordinances and expanded rental inspections to promote safety and meet the housing needs of residents at various life stages.
  • Leadership Style: Kari highlights her focus on building relationships and listening to the community, aiming to create positive, results-oriented governance. She encourages voters to join her campaign, emphasizing her commitment to New Brighton's progress.
  • Fiscal Responsibility: Gina stresses her commitment to reducing city spending and tax burdens, opposing what she describes as a current ideology-driven council agenda. She advocates for zero-based budgeting to assess city expenses annually, eliminating unnecessary spending.
  • Public Safety and Core Services: Gina promotes focusing on core services, including police, infrastructure, and maintenance of streets and parks, advocating for policies that support a safe community while avoiding additional programs or projects.
  • Housing and Development: She criticizes high-density housing projects, arguing they could negatively impact existing businesses and drive up city costs. She opposes rezoning efforts that could increase multifamily housing, preferring to maintain the city's suburban character.
  • Background: Gina draws on her prior council experience and business background, emphasizing her understanding of city operations and budgeting. She campaigns on bringing an objective and fiscally responsible approach to the council.
  • Local Involvement: Jeanne describes her extensive history of volunteering in New Brighton and serving on the Parks and Recreation and Planning Commissions. She emphasizes her long-term commitment to the community as both a resident and a real estate professional familiar with local issues.
  • Community Safety: Jeanne strongly supports New Brighton's Public Safety department, emphasizing the need to maintain high standards in hiring, training, and retention for police and fire departments to ensure a safe community.
  • Housing and Business Support: She is committed to addressing New Brighton's housing challenges, advocating for policies that balance housing needs with economic growth. She also emphasizes encouraging small business growth and supporting local organizations and schools.
  • Leadership and Vision: Jeanne highlights her history in New Brighton, focusing on preserving the city's character and quality of life. She promotes a vision of growth while keeping the community inclusive and welcoming for current and future residents.
  • Listening and Inclusivity: As a local pastor and educator, Jason prioritizes listening to residents and understanding their diverse needs. He believes that listening is the first step to effective governance and building a welcoming community.
  • Public Safety: Jason supports a well-resourced Public Safety department, focusing on adequate training and policy detail to keep the city safe. He also highlights the importance of equitable policies within the department.
  • Housing, Economic Growth, and Equity: Jason advocates for housing and economic growth policies that align with the city's needs and values, aiming to create opportunities for families, businesses, and community organizations. He highlights his role on the Equity Commission and his dedication to making New Brighton inclusive.
  • Action-Oriented: He promotes a balanced approach that combines listening and learning with decisive action, emphasizing his readiness to tackle complex issues in collaboration with community members. He encourages residents to get involved in creating a brighter future for New Brighton.
